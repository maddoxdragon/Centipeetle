`Centi Commands`
**PREFIXfetch**: Grab a simplistic text screenfetch from the host device
**PREFIXremfetch**: Grab a simplistic text screenfetch from a remote device
**PREFIXpacreb**: Runs `sudo pacman -Syu` to fully update your system and reboot
**PREFIXpacman**: Runs `sudo pacman -Syu` to fully update your system
**PREFIXreboot**: Reboots your system
**PREFIXcbook**: Personal command; uploads ~/Documents/cbook.png to the channel
**PREFIXd {NUMBER}**: Rolls dice
**PREFIXcredits**: Displays credits for the bot
**PREFIXsimon**: Has the bot repeat the containing message with `csimon` removed
**PREFIXaddresp {RESPONSE}**: Add a response to the response pool
**PREFIXyt {QUERY}**: Searches for a YouTube video and posts the URL
**PREFIXdlyt {QUERY}**: Searches for a video, downloads it and posts the file
**PREFIXreaminder {REMINDER}**: Adds a reminder to your reminders
**PREFIXremindclear**: Removes your reminders
**centi reminders**: List your reminders with their timestamps in a neat embed
**centi reminders plaintext**: List your reminders without a neat embed
**i love you centipeetle**: Show affection
**centipeetle, how was your day** or similar: Has the bot print a random response
**chaps** in message: Causes a Centipeetle response
View specifics and more at https://github.com/madgeraccoon/Centipeetle
